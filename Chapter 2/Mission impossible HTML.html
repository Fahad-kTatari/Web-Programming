Mission Impossible

Mission Impossible is a popular action film series created by Bruce Geller. The series follows the adventures of the Impossible Missions Force (IMF), a secret government agency tasked with completing dangerous and covert missions.

The series is known for its thrilling action sequences, intricate plots, and memorable characters.

Key Information

Director: Brian De Palma, John Woo, J.J. Abrams, Brad Bird, Christopher McQuarrie

Main Cast: Tom Cruise, Ving Rhames, Simon Pegg, Rebecca Ferguson, Michelle Monaghan

Release Date: 1996-present

Characters

  • Ethan Hunt - Played by Tom Cruise, he is the main protagonist and field agent of IMF.
  • Luther Stickell - Played by Ving Rhames, he is a skilled hacker and tech expert.
  • Benji Dunn - Played by Simon Pegg, he is a tech specialist and field agent.
  • Ilsa Faust - Played by Rebecca Ferguson, she is a former MI6 agent turned IMF operative.
